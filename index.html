FAQs

Are you NDIS-registered?

Yes. We’re registered and comply with the NDIS Practice Standards and Quality & Safeguards. Certificates available on request.

Do you provide weekend and overnight support?

Yes. We roster active and sleepover shifts to meet your needs.

Can I choose my support workers?

Absolutely. We match preferences and skills, and keep your team consistent.

Which areas do you cover?

Greater Sydney and select regional hubs. Ask us about your suburb.
